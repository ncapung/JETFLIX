2010 • 2h 28m Action | Sci-fi
Dom Cobb (Leonardo DiCaprio) is a thief with the rare ability to enter people's dreams and steal their secrets from their subconscious. His skill has made him a hot commodity in the world of corporate espionage but has also cost him everything he loves. Cobb gets a chance at redemption when he is offered a seemingly impossible task: Plant an idea in someone's mind. If he succeeds, it will be the perfect crime, but a dangerous enemy anticipates Cobb's every move.

Season 1
The Extractors S1 E1 • 1 June 2010 • 24m
In this episode, we are introduced to a team of skilled extractors led by Dominic Cobb. They specialize in stealing valuable information from the dreams of powerful individuals. They are hired by a wealthy businessman to retrieve a crucial piece of information locked within the mind of a rival executive.
The Architect S1 E2 • 8 June 2010 • 23m
The team recruits a talented architect named Ariadne, who possesses the rare ability to design intricate dreamscapes. She joins them for a new mission, which involves entering the dream of a renowned scientist to plant an idea that could change the course of scientific discovery.
The Forger S1 E3 • 25 June 2010 • 25m
The extractors seek the assistance of a master forger named Eames. Together, they delve into the mind of an art thief who possesses vital knowledge about a hidden treasure. Their objective is to extract the information while navigating the thief's elaborate subconscious defenses.
Limbo S1 E4 • 2 July 2010 • 23m
During a complex extraction, the team faces a setback, leading one of their members to fall into a deep state of limbo—a subconscious realm where time is distorted. The rest of the team must enter this treacherous limbo to rescue their comrade before they become trapped in an eternal nightmare.
The Projection S1 E5 • 9 July 2010 • 24m
A powerful organization becomes aware of the extractors' activities and sends a specialized team known as "projections" after them. These skilled adversaries can manipulate the dream world to their advantage. The extractors must outsmart and evade their pursuers while attempting to complete a critical mission.
Inception S1 E4 • 2 July 2010 • 29m
In the final episode, Cobb and his team embark on their most daring mission yet: inception. They are hired by a desperate client to plant an idea deep within the subconscious of a powerful individual, aiming to influence his decision-making in the real world. However, their task is met with unexpected challenges and dangerous consequences that threaten to unravel their entire operation.
